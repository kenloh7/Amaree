✦ Our Story

Born in Malaysia,
Made for Every Woman

Amarée started with one question: why should any Malaysian woman have to compromise on her outfit choices? We built the answer.

🌺

A Brand Born from Frustration

In 2021, our founder Mei Lin stood in a Pavilion changing room holding a backless dress she loved, knowing she couldn't buy it because she had no solution for support. That moment sparked Amarée.

After 18 months of R&D with a team of Malaysian designers, material scientists, and real women across the country — we launched Amarée in 2023. Our bras are engineered for Malaysia's unique climate: the humidity, the heat, the long hours of wearing.

Today, over 15,000 Malaysian women trust Amarée for their most important moments — weddings, graduations, galas, and everyday boldness. We ship from Kuala Lumpur to every corner of Malaysia, same day.

"Wear what you love. We'll take care of the rest."

— Mei Lin, Founder & CEO
